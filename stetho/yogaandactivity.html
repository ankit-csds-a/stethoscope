Yoga & Activity Plan for Respiratory Health

Effective exercises and activities to support recovery from respiratory diseases.

Breathing Exercises

  • Diaphragmatic Breathing: Sit comfortably, place one hand on your chest and the other on your abdomen. Breathe deeply through your nose, allowing your abdomen to rise, then exhale slowly through your mouth.
  • Pursed Lip Breathing: Inhale through your nose for 2 counts, purse your lips, and exhale slowly for 4 counts. This keeps airways open and slows breathing.
  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4 counts, exhale for 4 counts, and hold again for 4 counts. Improves control over breathing.

Yoga Poses for Lung Health

  • Cat-Cow Pose: Alternate between arching and rounding your back. Improves chest flexibility and lung expansion.
  • Bridge Pose: Lie on your back, bend knees, and lift your hips. Stretches the chest and supports lung health.
  • Child's Pose: Sit on your knees, fold forward, and extend arms. Relieves tension in the chest and improves breathing.
  • Seated Forward Bend: Sit with legs extended, and slowly bend forward. Stretches the diaphragm and boosts lung function.

Physical Activity Plan

  • Walking: Begin with short walks and gradually increase the distance.
  • Swimming: A low-impact activity that enhances lung function and endurance.
  • Cycling: Strengthens respiratory muscles and improves overall fitness.
  • Strength Training: Incorporate light weights with controlled breathing for lung health.
